Our Story

Abuha, a coffee shop established in 2020, came to life due to my passion for coffee. Initially, I started by handcrafting coffee for my family and closest friends. Abuha's focus is on selling various high-quality coffees. Through hard work and dedication, this business has grown beyond a family venture, and now we aim to expand our reach by venturing into the online world. We take pride in offering select coffees to coffee lovers worldwide. With distinctive flavors and meticulous production processes, Abuha is committed to delivering an unforgettable coffee experience to each of our customers.
